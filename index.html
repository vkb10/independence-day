History of Indian Independence

India gained independence from British rule on 15th August 1947 after a long struggle led by great leaders such as Mahatma Gandhi, Jawaharlal Nehru, Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, Subhas Chandra Bose and countless freedom fighters.

The movement combined non-violent protests, civil disobedience, and sacrifices of millions. The tricolour flag became the symbol of hope and unity.
